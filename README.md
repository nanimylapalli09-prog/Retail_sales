# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of retail business performance by analyzing sales, customer activity, product categories, cities, payment methods, and sales representatives.

By converting raw transactional data into meaningful visualizations, the dashboard enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions.

##  Key Business Insights

- The business generated **₹15.26 million** in total sales from over **1,200 customer transactions**, indicating strong sales performance.
- **Delhi** recorded the highest sales, making it the top-performing market, while **Hyderabad** generated the lowest revenue.
- **Clothing** contributed the highest revenue among all product categories, followed by Electronics.
- Sales showed noticeable fluctuations across months, suggesting the influence of seasonal demand and promotional campaigns.
- **Net Banking** and **Cash** were the most preferred payment methods, whereas **Card** payments were used less frequently.
- **Bob** achieved the highest sales among all sales representatives.
- **Mouse, Jacket, and Headphones** were the highest-selling products by quantity, reflecting strong customer demand.
- Grocery products generated comparatively lower revenue, presenting opportunities for targeted promotions, bundling, and cross-selling strategies.
